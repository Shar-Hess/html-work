Jill Jones

Position: Coach

Bio: Coach Jill Jones brings over 15 years of experience to the team, having led several high school and collegiate teams to championship victories. Her strategic mindset and dedication to player development have earned her a reputation as one of the top coaches in the region. Jill is known for her motivational leadership style and her ability to build strong, cohesive teams. Her passion for the game and commitment to excellence inspire players to reach their full potential both on and off the field.







John Smith

Position: Assistant Coach

Bio: Assistant Coach John Smith is a former professional athlete with a deep understanding of game tactics and player conditioning. With 10 years of coaching experience, John specializes in skills development and game strategy. His hands-on approach and technical expertise help players improve their individual skills while contributing to the overall team strategy. John is also known for his positive attitude and ability to connect with players, making him a valuable mentor and resource for the team.







Bill Jensen

Position: Team Manager

Bio: Team Manager Bill Jensen is the organizational backbone of the team, ensuring that everything runs smoothly both on and off the field. With a background in sports management and event coordination, Bill handles scheduling, logistics, and communication with efficiency and professionalism. His attention to detail and problem-solving skills ensure that the team is well-prepared for every game and practice. Bill's commitment to creating a supportive and well-organized environment plays a crucial role in the team's success.
